About Revvens

Revvens is a software-as-a-service (SaaS) platform designed for healthcare providers, pharmacies, clinics, and medical businesses. The platform enables digital operations including inventory management, billing, prescriptions, payments, analytics, and secure customer communication.

Services Offered

  • Pharmacy Inventory & Sales Management
  • GST-Compliant Billing & Invoicing
  • Digital Prescription Management
  • Multi-Tenant ERP for Clinics & Pharmacies
  • Payment Integrations (Razorpay, Stripe)
  • Customer Communication via WhatsApp, Email & SMS
  • Reporting, Analytics & Compliance Tools
