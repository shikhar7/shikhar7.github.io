Frequently Asked Questions

What is Revvens?
Revvens is a pharmacy ERP and Paperless Prescription Management platform for healthcare providers and pharmacies.
How do I create an account?
Sign up via the Revvens portal with accurate business details for verification.
How is patient data protected?
We use encryption, secure storage, and comply with NDHM standards for data privacy.
Can I access my prescription history?
Yes, past prescriptions are accessible via the MediCore dashboard.
How do I contact support?
Email support@revvens.com for assistance.
