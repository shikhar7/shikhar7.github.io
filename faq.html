Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. What is Revvens?
Revvens is a comprehensive pharmacy ERP and Paperless Prescription Management platform for the Indian healthcare ecosystem.
2. How do I create an account?
You can sign up via the Revvens web portal or through authorized partners. Ensure you provide accurate details for verification.
3. How is patient data protected?
We use strong encryption, secure storage, and follow NDHM and HIPAA-aligned standards to ensure privacy and data protection.
4. Can I access my prescription history?
Yes, you can view past prescriptions and sales through the MediCore dashboard once logged in.
5. How do I contact support?
Contact our support team via the Help section in your dashboard or email support@revvens.com.
